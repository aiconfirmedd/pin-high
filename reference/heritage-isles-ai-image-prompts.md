## Usage in App

These prompts generate the **hole background images** used in the GPS Interactive Hole Map
view inside CaddieMode. Each image becomes the canvas layer underneath the draggable
tee/bullseye markers.

**Integration target:** `src/components/CaddieMode/HoleMap.tsx`  
**Reference in course data:** `HOLE_IMAGE_URLS` in `src/data/courses/heritage-isles/index.ts`

Suggested image spec for app use:
- Size: **1080×720** (landscape) or **720×1080** (portrait)
- Format: **WebP**, quality 85
- Export with course/hole naming: `heritage-isles-hole-01.webp` through `heritage-isles-hole-18.webp`
- Store in: `public/courses/heritage-isles/`
